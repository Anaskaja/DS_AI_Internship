# Customer Analytics - Data Dictionary
**Total Rows:** 255 (250 unique customers after cleaning)
**Total Columns:** 14

### Feature Descriptions:
* **CustomerID:** Unique identifier for the shopper.
* **Age / Gender / City / Education / MaritalStatus:** Customer demographics. 
* **AnnualIncome:** Yearly income in dollars.
* **SpendingScore:** A proprietary metric (1-100) scoring customer value.
* **YearsEmployed:** Number of years the customer has been working.
* **PurchaseFrequency:** Total number of purchases made.
* **OnlineVisitsPerMonth:** Traffic metrics for the e-commerce store.
* **ReturnedItems:** Count of items returned by the customer.
* **PreferredDevice:** How the user browses (Laptop, Desktop, Mobile, Tablet).
* **LastPurchaseAmount:** Value of their most recent transaction.
